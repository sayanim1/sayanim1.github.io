Hi, I’m Sayani Mallick.

I design, build, and deploy data‑intensive systems and machine learning solutions that solve real business problems.

I am a Masters in Computer Science student at University of Texas at Dallas, USA, graduating in May 2027.

Before University of Texas at Dallas, I worked as a Software Engineer at Flipkart in India. I completed my undergraduate education at Birla Institute of Technology and Science, K.K. Birla Goa Campus in Electronics and Communication. I love solving real-world problems with engineering and logic.

On the engineering side, I’ve worked on high-traffic storefront pipelines, Kafka–Spark–Hadoop data flows, and Java/Python services that ship to production. On the AI side, I’ve built seizure detection models, multi-agent LLM workflows, and an exergame agent that adapts to your heart rate and movement in real time.

I’m happiest when I’m debugging messy pipelines, turning vague ideas into shipped features, or hacking on weird ML projects at 2 a.m. If you’re working on backend, machine learning, or agentic AI systems, we will probably have a lot to talk about.
